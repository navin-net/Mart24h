Frequently Asked Questions

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs. Standard shipping (5-7 business days) is free for orders over $50, and $4.99 for orders under $50. Expedited shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $9.99, and Next-Day shipping is available for $19.99. International shipping rates vary by country and can be calculated at checkout.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Some items, such as personalized products or intimate apparel, are not eligible for return. To initiate a return, please visit your account dashboard or contact our customer service team.

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package. You can also view your order status and tracking information in your account dashboard under "My Orders."
